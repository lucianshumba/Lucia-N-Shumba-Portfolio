// 01 — About

Who I Am

I'm Lucia Nomatter Shumba — a data science and analytics professional with over eight years of experience in statistical modeling, risk analysis, advanced analytics, and machine learning. I hold a Master of Official Statistics (specialized in Agriculture) from the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC).

My work sits at the intersection of rigorous quantitative methods and real-world impact. I've built predictive models for labor economics, constructed enterprise-grade healthcare data warehouses, and conducted econometric research that informs food security policy in Southern Africa.

I believe the best analyses are those that don't just answer the question asked, but surface the questions nobody thought to ask. That's the standard I hold myself to.
